Seduction of Patrick
1979, 28 minutes

This loosely scripted piece is a collaboration between Auder and writer Gary Indiana. It is a tale of unrequited love revolving around Gary's attraction for Patrick, a French composer and pianist.

Attempts by Indiana's friends to pique Patrick's interest in Indiana prove futile as they too find themselves attracted to Patrick whose sexuality is unclear. Before the standard plot devices of betrayal and incest kick-in, Viva has an outstanding cameo recalling the best of her work with Warhol.

Shot in the studio of Swiss painter Olivier Mosset, Seduction's all star cast includes Gary Indiana, Patrick Selinger, Colette, Florence Lambert, Taylor Mead, Cookie Mueller, Ruby Lynn Rainer, Marcia Resnick, Terryl Robinson, Robert Smith, Anita Sarko, Willoughby Sharp, Viva and Bill Rice.
